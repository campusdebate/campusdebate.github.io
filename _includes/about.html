About

CampusDebate is a software company founded for the purpose of bringing more student voices into the public sphere.

Amazing conversations and debates happen daily on college and university campuses, but they are usually invisible outside of the dining halls and dormitories.

Our goal is to bring student ideas, on topics ranging from American politics to global warming, onto a platform that allows everyone to view the discussion, provide feedback, and build a bridge between the campus and the community. All voting data will be made freely available to the college or university at which the debate took place.
